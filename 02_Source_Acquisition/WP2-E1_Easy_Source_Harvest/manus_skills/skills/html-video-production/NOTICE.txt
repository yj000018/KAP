The bundled Pixabay sound-effects library is also copied to `assets/sfx/`.

Modifications
-------------
Upstream files were modified to bring media generation under the Manus
Three-Tier Priority Model (Tier 1 Manus-native `generate_speech` /
`generate_music` / `generate_image`; Tier 2 permitted local open-source
fallbacks such as whisper.cpp transcription, U2-Net background removal, and
the bundled Pixabay SFX; Tier 3 prohibited paths — `npx hyperframes init`
auto-pull, `hyperframes lambda *` paid cloud render, and the HeyGen-paid
branch). The changes are: a "MANUS OVERRIDE" banner prepended to every
bundled `SKILL.md`; two lines rewritten in `hyperframes-cli/SKILL.md`
(scaffold + cloud render); and inline override markers inserted before the
orchestrator-invoking commands in five workflow `SKILL.md` files. The
complete summary is in `_manus-overrides/modifications.md`, satisfying the
modification disclosure requirement of Apache License 2.0 §4(b).

The following Manus-authored materials are added in this package:

  - SKILL.md (top-level)

  - NOTICE.txt (this file)
  - _manus-overrides/media-generation.md
  - _manus-overrides/modifications.md
  - assets/sfx/ (copy of the upstream Pixabay SFX library, Pixabay Content
    License; see references/hyperframes-media/assets/sfx/CREDITS.md)
