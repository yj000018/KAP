## Executive Summary

User requested comparison of large ficus plants (>150cm) available for Swiss delivery, specifically to Haute-Nendaz. Research revealed online options are limited for live plants of this size, with artificial plants being more readily available. Shifted focus to physical garden centers in Valais and up to Lausanne region.


## Context & Intent

Home decoration project requiring large live ficus plant for delivery to Haute-Nendaz, Switzerland


## What Was Done

Comprehensive research of online plant retailers with Swiss delivery, followed by identification of physical garden centers in Valais and Lausanne regions with contact details and recommendations


## Outputs Produced

- [comparative_analysis] Online Ficus Retailers Comparison — Detailed comparison of 5 Swiss online plant retailers with availability and pricing
- [regional_directory] Valais-Lausanne Garden Centers — List of 7 physical garden centers with contact details, distances, and recommendations

## Key Decisions & Validations

- Excluded artificial plants per user preference
- Expanded search to physical retailers due to online limitations
- Prioritized Valais locations for proximity to Haute-Nendaz

## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- Systematic comparison approach
- Geographic proximity prioritization
- Including promotional information
Failed / suboptimal:

- Online retailers have limited large live plant inventory
- Delivery constraints for oversized plants
Discoveries:

- Green-Bubble.com has best selection but no Swiss delivery
- Point Vert Conthey has current 20% discount on indoor plants

## Challenges & Blockers

- Online retailers don't deliver large live plants to Switzerland
- No detailed online catalogs for physical stores requiring phone calls

## Open Questions

- Actual availability and pricing at recommended garden centers
- Delivery options from physical stores to Haute-Nendaz
