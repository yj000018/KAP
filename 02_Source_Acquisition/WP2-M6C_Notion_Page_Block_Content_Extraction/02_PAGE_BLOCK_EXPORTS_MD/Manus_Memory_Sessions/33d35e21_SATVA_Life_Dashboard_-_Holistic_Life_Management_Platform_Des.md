## Executive Summary

Yannick conceptualizes and develops SATVA, a comprehensive life management dashboard based on the 7 chakras model. Starting with research on existing platforms like Kubera, the session evolves into creating a complete architecture document and building three iterations of a visual prototype. The dashboard integrates all life dimensions (finances, health, career, relationships, learning, vision, spirituality) with AI assistance throughout.


## Context & Intent

Yannick was inspired by Kubera's financial dashboard and wanted to create a holistic life management platform covering all aspects of existence, not just finances. The goal was to have a single morning dashboard showing status, tasks, progress, and insights across all life dimensions.


## What Was Done

Complete market research, architecture design using 7 chakras model (TERRA/FLOW/FORGE/NEXUS/VOICE/SIGHT/SOURCE), and three iterative prototypes of the SATVA dashboard with increasingly sophisticated visual design, data density, and AI integration.


## Outputs Produced

- [document] SATVA_Architecture.md — Complete platform architecture based on 7 life layers/chakras
- [diagram] Architecture Diagram — Visual representation of SATVA platform structure
- [prototype] SATVA Dashboard v3 — Interactive web prototype with colorful gradients, dense layout, AI panels

## Key Decisions & Validations

- Adopt 7 chakras model as organizing principle for life management
- Name platform SATVA (Sanskrit for clarity/harmony)
- Use 'Cosmos Interior' design theme with chakra colors
- Integrate AI assistant contextually in each dimension
- Prioritize density and visual richness over minimalism

## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- 7 chakras framework provides clear structure
- Market gap exists for holistic life dashboards
- Iterative prototype development with user feedback
Failed / suboptimal:

- Initial dark theme too depressing
- First layouts not dense enough for desktop
- Missing actionable buttons and AI integration
Discoveries:

- No existing platform unifies all life aspects
- Need for morning ritual dashboard
- AI can enhance every life dimension

## Challenges & Blockers

- Visual design balance between information density and aesthetics
- Data integration from multiple sources (Kubera, Apple Health, Notion)
- Making complex life management feel simple and inviting

## Open Questions

- How to connect real data sources vs simulated data?
- What's the optimal refresh frequency for different life metrics?
- How to gamify life improvement without oversimplification?

## Next Steps

- Add Timeline view for cross-dimensional life events
- Connect real data sources (Notion, Kubera, health apps)
