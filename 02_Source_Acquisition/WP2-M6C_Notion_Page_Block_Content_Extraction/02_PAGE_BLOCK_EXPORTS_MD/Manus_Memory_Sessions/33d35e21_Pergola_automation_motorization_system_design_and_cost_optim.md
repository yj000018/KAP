## Lessons Learned

- Cost optimization through creative mechanical solutions
- Comparative analysis of multiple vendor solutions
Failed / suboptimal:

- Initial single-roof assumption required complete redesign when scope expanded
- Credit limitation prevented website deployment completion
Discoveries:

- Mechanical transmission can reduce motorization costs by over 50% for multi-zone systems
- GM120 motors offer excellent price-performance ratio for DIY pergola automation
