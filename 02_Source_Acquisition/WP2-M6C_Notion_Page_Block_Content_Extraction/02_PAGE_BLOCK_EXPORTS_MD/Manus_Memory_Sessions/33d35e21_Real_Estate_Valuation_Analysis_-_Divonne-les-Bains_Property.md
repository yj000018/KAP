## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- Systematic challenge of initial assumptions improved accuracy
- Use of real market listings provided concrete anchoring points
- Multi-perspective approach (buyer/seller) revealed true price range
Failed / suboptimal:

- Initial estimates lacked sufficient market reality checks
- Renovation value was significantly overestimated
- Required work costs were underestimated initially
Discoveries:

- Market for 20+ year apartments more challenging than assumed
- Comparable 'retour vente' property indicates pricing difficulties
- Cumulative percentage calculations needed for accurate market adjustments
