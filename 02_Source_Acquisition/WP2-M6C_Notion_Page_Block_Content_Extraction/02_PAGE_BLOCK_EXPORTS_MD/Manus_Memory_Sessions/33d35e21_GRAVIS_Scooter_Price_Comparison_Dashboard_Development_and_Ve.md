## Executive Summary

User requested creation of a comprehensive price comparison dashboard for GRAVIS mobility scooters with specific requirements (lithium battery, captain seat, red color, delivery to France). Manus developed multiple iterations of an interactive dashboard, researched 9+ vendors including French suppliers, and created detailed comparison tables. The project evolved from basic research to sophisticated filtering/sorting capabilities, culminating in refined vendor selection and professional email templates for purchase inquiries.


## Context & Intent

User needed to purchase a GRAVIS mobility scooter with specific technical requirements and wanted comprehensive market research to identify the best vendors and pricing options for immediate acquisition.


## What Was Done

Created multiple iterations of interactive web dashboards, researched 9+ vendors across different countries, analyzed product specifications, created comparison tables highlighting differences, translated documents to French, and prepared professional email templates for vendor inquiries.

## Key Decisions & Validations

- Prioritized French vendors while maintaining international options
- Removed UK vendors per user preference
- Focused on vendors under €3000 budget constraint
- Verified lithium battery availability and corrected misrepresentations
- Added anti-theft alarm and puncture-proof tire requirements
- Selected 4 final vendors for purchase inquiries

## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- Interactive dashboard with filtering/sorting provided excellent user experience
- Iterative refinement based on user feedback improved accuracy
- Comprehensive vendor research identified multiple viable options
Failed / suboptimal:

- Initial research incorrectly assumed lithium batteries without verification
- First dashboard lacked sufficient vendor coverage
- Some vendor specifications required deeper verification
Discoveries:

- Many vendors don't clearly specify lithium vs lead-acid batteries
- French market has good coverage for mobility scooters
- Price range varies significantly (€2,340-€3,999) for similar features
- Anti-theft alarms and puncture-proof tires are not standard features

## Challenges & Blockers

- Credit limitation interrupted research process briefly
- Difficulty verifying specific technical specifications from vendor websites
- Inconsistent product information across different vendor sites
- Some vendor email addresses required verification for purchase inquiries

## Open Questions

- Which vendor will provide the best response to the inquiry emails?
- Are there additional technical specifications that need verification?
- Should warranty terms be compared more thoroughly before purchase?
- Are there seasonal pricing variations to consider?

## Next Steps
