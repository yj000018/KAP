## Key Decisions & Validations

- Filtered to only include models with actual massage jets (eliminated 109+ basic bathtubs)
- Applied strict size constraint of 180cm maximum length
- Required 2-person capacity as mandatory criterion
- Split into two categories: powerful spa jets vs whirlpool massage systems
- Selected 15 premium brands for comprehensive coverage

## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- Systematic brand-by-brand research approach
- Interactive HTML table with advanced filtering capabilities
- Clear categorization between spa-grade and whirlpool systems
- Technical specification standardization across brands
Failed / suboptimal:

- Initial scope too broad with basic bathtubs included
- Size constraints eliminated many premium spa options
- Market for 2-person compact spas extremely limited
Discoveries:

- Only 2 true spa models meet all criteria (size + capacity + jets)
- RIHO dominates with 50+ models but no jets
- Whirlpool market much larger than spa market for compact sizes
- European brands focus on aesthetics, American/Canadian on functionality

## Challenges & Blockers

- Extremely limited market for compact 2-person spas under 180cm
- Technical specification inconsistencies across manufacturers
- Price information not always readily available
- Distinguishing between air-bath systems and actual massage jets
