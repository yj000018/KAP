## Executive Summary

User requested generation of photorealistic 3D images and technical models of a swimming pool based on architectural plans, to be placed in front of their house in Taormina. Manus analyzed detailed architectural blueprints showing a kidney-shaped pool with specific dimensions (9.0m x 4.5m) and varying depths (40cm to 140cm). Multiple deliverables were produced including photorealistic renderings, technical 3D models, and complete documentation with precise measurements extracted from the original plans.


## Context & Intent

## What Was Done
