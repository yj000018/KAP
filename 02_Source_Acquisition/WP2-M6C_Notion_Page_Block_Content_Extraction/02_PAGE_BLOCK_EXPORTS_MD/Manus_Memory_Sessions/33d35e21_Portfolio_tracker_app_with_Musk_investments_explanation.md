## Executive Summary

Built a comprehensive portfolio tracking app with auto price fetching for Tesla/SpaceX/xAI investments, showing $300k invested returning ~$1M (+237%). Added French explanatory page to make complex investment story accessible to non-finance audiences, particularly targeting user's wife as audience.


## Context & Intent

User wanted a simple app to track portfolio performance with auto price search, editable acquisition dates/prices, and animated graphs. Later requested adding explanatory content in accessible French to explain the investment story to non-technical audiences.


## What Was Done

Created full-stack portfolio tracker with dark terminal aesthetic, auto TSLA price fetching via Yahoo Finance, private valuations for SpaceX/xAI, inline editing capabilities, animated charts, and added comprehensive French explanatory page styled as accessible magazine content with two-tab navigation.


## Outputs Produced

- [web_app] Portfolio Tracker — React app with portfolio table, price fetching, inline editing, animated charts

## Key Decisions & Validations
