Comprehensive testing of HeyGen MCP connector for AI avatar video generation. Successfully demonstrated core functionality including avatar discovery, metadata retrieval, and video creation. Generated two test videos: one in English with Matthew avatar (successful) and one attempting French poetry recitation with female avatar (partial success due to avatar availability issues). User requested testing of HeyGen connector to understand its capabilities, then wanted to create a video with specific female avatar reciting French poetry from Cavafy's Ithaca


## What Was Done

Tested HeyGen MCP connector tools, fetched avatar catalog data, retrieved detailed metadata for specific avatars, generated test videos with different avatars and languages, monitored video generation status, and attempted multilingual content creation
