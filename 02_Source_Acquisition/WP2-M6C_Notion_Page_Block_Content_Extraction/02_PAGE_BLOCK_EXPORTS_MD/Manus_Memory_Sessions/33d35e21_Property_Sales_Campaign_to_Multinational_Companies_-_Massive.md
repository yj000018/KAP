## Executive Summary

Comprehensive real estate marketing campaign for selling an apartment in Divonne-les-Bains to multinational companies in the Lake Geneva region. Successfully executed massive email outreach to 55 organizations (UN, WHO, pharmaceutical companies, banks, luxury brands, international schools) targeting expatriates and international staff. Campaign reached ~75,000 employees with personalized bilingual messages, achieved 96.4% delivery rate, and included multi-channel follow-up strategy via web forms and LinkedIn.


## Context & Intent

Help sell Yannick's mother's apartment in Divonne-les-Bains by targeting expatriates working for multinational companies in the Geneva region, bypassing traditional real estate agencies for direct owner-to-buyer sales.


## What Was Done

Created comprehensive contact database, sent 55 personalized bilingual emails across 5 batches, handled delivery errors and corrections, researched alternative contact methods, prepared LinkedIn outreach strategy, monitored responses, and maintained real-time tracking spreadsheet.


## Outputs Produced

- [email campaign] 55 personalized emails — Bilingual messages to HR departments with brochure attachments
- [contact database] Excel tracking file — Complete contact list with status tracking and response monitoring
- [strategy document] LinkedIn outreach plan — 55 HR contacts with personalized message templates
- [market analysis] Reach assessment — Estimated 16,500 qualified targets from 75,000 total employees

## Key Decisions & Validations

- Target multinational companies with high expatriate populations
- Use bilingual messaging approach
- Implement multi-channel strategy (email, web forms, LinkedIn)
- Include both contact emails as BCC for transparency
- Prioritize organizations by employee count and expatriate likelihood

## Lessons Learned

Worked well:

- 96.4% email delivery success rate
- Personalized messages for known contacts
- Real-time error detection and correction
- Comprehensive target research and categorization
Failed / suboptimal:

- LinkedIn Premium required for direct messaging
- Many company websites have anti-bot protections
- Some HR emails are automated/monitored systems
Discoveries:

- International organizations most accessible via email
- Luxury brands and pharmaceuticals have extensive expatriate networks
- Swiss companies often prefer phone contact over web forms

## Challenges & Blockers

- LinkedIn Premium subscription needed for direct messaging
- CAPTCHA requirements on web forms
- Company website access restrictions
- Automated email systems blocking external messages

## Open Questions

- Response rates and conversion timing
- Effectiveness of web form vs email approach
- LinkedIn connection acceptance rates
- Optimal follow-up sequence and timing
