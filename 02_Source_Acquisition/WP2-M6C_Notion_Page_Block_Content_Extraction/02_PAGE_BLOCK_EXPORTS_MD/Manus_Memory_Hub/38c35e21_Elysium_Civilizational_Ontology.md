## Vision & Objectif

Elysium is a universal, fractal civilizational ontology — a minimal, elegant map of human, social, and planetary evolution. It maps ALL dimensions of civilization through 3 Scales and 7 Foundations.


## Architecture Validee


### 3 Scales

- Human — Individual, personal, interior
- Society — Collective, institutional, communal
- Civilization — Planetary, species-level, epochal

### 7 Foundations

1. Foundation (Material Base) — Energy, Water, Habitat, Infrastructure, Mobility, Materials
1. Vitality (Life and Ecology) — Health, Food, Biodiversity, Ecosystems, Regeneration
1. Agency (Economy and Production) — Economy, Work, Finance, Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Production
1. Cohesion (Community and Culture) — Community, Relationships, Culture, Art, Media, Identity
1. Governance (Coordination and Law) — Law/Justice, Democracy, Institutions, Security, Communication
1. Vision (Knowledge and Foresight) — Education, Science, Foresight, Systems Thinking, Technology
1. Consciousness (Meaning and Purpose) — Ethics, Spirituality, Purpose, Worldview, Civilizational Direction
