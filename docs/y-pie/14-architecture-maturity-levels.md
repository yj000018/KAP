# Architecture Maturity Levels (AML)

## Purpose

Prevent promising research ideas from being mistaken for production-ready capabilities.

AML applies to every Y-PIE capability, model, workflow and architectural proposal.

## Levels

### AML-0 — Idea

A concept exists but has no explicit problem statement, owner, evidence or validation path.

Required to advance:
- clear problem statement;
- intended user or system value;
- known risks;
- named research owner.
