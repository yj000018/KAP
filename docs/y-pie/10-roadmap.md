# Roadmap and Milestones

## Program structure

Y-PIE evolves from a safe visual indexing MVP into a full visual cognitive system.

## Phase 0 — Canon and research foundation

Deliverables:
- vision and principles;
- KAP boundary and contracts;
- ontology;
- data and provenance model;
- governance;
- evaluation plan;
- ADR/RFC process;
- implementation backlog.

Exit criterion: architecture review completed and no unresolved ownership conflict with KAP.

## Phase 1 — PIE Core MVP

Scope:
- Immich read adapter;
- stable asset registry;
- metadata and technical analysis;
- functional categories;
- captions and dominant colors;
- PostgreSQL persistence;
- safe tag/album write-back;
- batch runner and observability.

No deletion, no autonomous archive.

## Phase 2 — Similarity and series

- generic embeddings;
- exact and near duplicates;
- visual/style/concept neighbors;
- cluster generation;
- series detection;
- best-variant recommendations;
- corpus map prototype.

## Phase 3 — Art and Creative Intelligence

- specialist art ontology;
- composition and abstraction analysis;
- corpus-relative originality;
- symbol and archetype hypotheses;
- series roles;
- hidden-gem detection;
- project-alignment analysis;
- curation workbench.

## Phase 4 — Memory Intelligence

- people/place/event context from KAP;
- probable emotional significance;
- biographical uniqueness;
- protected-memory policies;
- best-memory variant selection;
- narrative and retrospective generation.

## Phase 5 — Visual DNA

- trait extraction;
- creative periods;
- trend and rupture detection;
- project-specific visual languages;
- personal similarity explanation;
- Visual DNA dashboard;
- contribution to Personal Knowledge Genome.

## Phase 6 — KAP graph integration

- evidence-bundle publication;
- entity-resolution feedback;
- cross-modal relations;
- visual-to-text and visual-to-project reasoning;
- KRE and ART query interfaces.

## Phase 7 — Curator and Narrative Engine

- automated collections;
- portfolios;
- books and exhibitions;
- moodboards;
- visual storytelling;
- collection critique and gap analysis.

## Phase 8 — Safe pruning

- calibrated delete-candidate models;
- quarantine workflows;
- policy simulation;
- false-positive monitoring;
- verified backup gates;
- reversible automation.

## Phase 9 — Multimodal expansion

Generalize contracts for video, audio, documents, spatial and sensor perception while keeping KAP as the canonical knowledge layer.

## MVP success criteria

- >90% precision on screenshots/documents/product-research classes;
- <1% protected-memory false delete-candidate rate in tests;
- reproducible analyses with full provenance;
- less than 10% manual category correction on calibrated corpus;
- useful Immich review queues;
- no original-file mutation.
